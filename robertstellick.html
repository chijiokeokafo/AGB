Robert Stellick

Robert is building a broad litigation practice.

Robert graduated with distinction from the University of Toronto Faculty of Law. After graduating, he articled at the Toronto office of Dentons Canada LLP and worked as a judicial law clerk at the Court of Appeal for Ontario.

Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber, Robert completed a Master of Laws at the University of Cambridge on the Right Honourable Paul Martin Sr. Scholarship, graduating with First Class Honours. While at Cambridge, he coached the men's varsity ice hockey team. 

Before returning to Canada, Robert was selected as a Pegasus Scholar, working within barristers' chambers in London, England.
