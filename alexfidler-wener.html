Alex Fidler-Wener

Alex Fidler-Wener is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Alex maintains a broad civil litigation practice. Her areas of practice include corporate and commercial disputes, professional regulation and liability, securities litigation, administrative proceedings, internal investigations, and civil sexual assault claims. Alex has represented clients before administrative tribunals, all levels of court in Ontario, and the Supreme Court of Canada.

Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Alex practiced as an associate at Wardle Daley Bernstein Bieber LLP. Alex spent her articles clerking for the judges of the Court of Appeal for Ontario. She spent her summers as a law student with Blake, Cassels & Graydon LLP and the Legal Services Branch of Ontario’s Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care.

While studying at the University of Toronto Faculty of Law, Alex received a number of awards recognizing her overall academic achievement, including the McCarthy, Tetrault Prize and the Fasken Martineau DuMoulin LLP Award. She also received awards recognizing her specific achievements in health and administrative law.

Alex is committed to the provision of pro bono legal services. While in law school, Alex worked as a case worker at Advocates for Injured Workers and a researcher with the Faculty’s International Human Rights Program. Alex is currently an active participant in Pro Bono Ontario’s Divisional Court Amicus Program and Free Legal Advice Hotline.
