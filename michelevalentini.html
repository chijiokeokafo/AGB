Michele Valentini

Michele Valentini is an associate at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Prior to joining AGB, Michele served as a judicial law clerk to judges of the Court of Appeal for Ontario. She completed her articles at the Ministry of the Attorney General, Crown Law Office - Civil (CLOC), which is the central civil litigation office for the government of Ontario.

Michele completed her law degree at the University of Ottawa, where she graduated with high distinction. While studying at uOttawa, Michele was a course tutor for the first-year public law and constitutional law course and was actively involved in the Faculty of Law's Legal Writing Academy.

Michele began volunteering with the Ontario Justice Education Network (OJEN) while in law school. In 2017, she received the Tate Herbert Memorial Post-Secondary Award, recognizing her contributions to OJEN's justice education initiatives.
