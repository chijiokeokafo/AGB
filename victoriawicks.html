Victoria Wicks

Victoria Wicks is an associate at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Before joining the firm, Victoria completed her articles as a judicial law clerk at the Court of Appeal for Ontario. She spent her summers as a law student with Blake, Cassels & Graydon LLP in Toronto and with the Bingham Centre for the Rule of Law in the United Kingdom.

Victoria earned her law degree at the University of British Columbia. During law school, Victoria worked at the Indigenous Community Legal Clinic and won the inaugural Competition Law Moot. She also received various awards for extracurricular leadership and academic achievement, including course prizes for public, tort, and administrative law.
