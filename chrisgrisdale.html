Chris Grisdale

Chris is building a broad litigation practice.

Chris has acted in matters before the British Columbia Supreme Court, the British Columbia Court of Appeal, the Ontario Superior Court, and the Ontario Court of Appeal. Before joining the firm, Chris was a litigation associate at a national firm and clerked for four judges of the British Columbia Supreme Court.

During law school, Chris won several prizes for academic merit and worked for the Canadian Centre for Policy Alternatives and the United Food and Commercial Workers Union on law reform. He has published op-eds in the Toronto Star and the Globe and Mail.
