Eleanor Vaughan

Eleanor Vaughan is an associate at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP. She maintains a broad civil litigation practice, representing clients in a variety of corporate and commercial disputes.

Prior to joining the firm, Eleanor practiced as a litigation associate with Osler Hoskin & Harcourt LLP, where she also completed her articles.

While studying at the University of Toronto Faculty of Law, Eleanor was active in the mooting program and completed an International Human Rights Program internship in Hong Kong. She also recently completed a Master of Laws at Université Lyon III in France.

Eleanor is an active volunteer with Pro Bono Law Ontario.
