Whitehouse v. BDO Canada LLP

UPDATE/NEXT STEPS:

The Statement of Claim was filed on July 20, 2017. BDO Canada LLP delivered a Notice of Intent to Defend on November 29, 2017.

The parties attended an early stage mediation. No settlement was reached.

The certification motion was heard by the Honourable Justice Paul Perell on December 9 and 10, 2019. On January 8, 2020, the Court released its decision denying certification of the class action. We are considering next steps, and will provide a further update shortly.
