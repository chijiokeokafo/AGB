Whitehouse v. BDO Canada LLP

UPDATE/NEXT STEPS:

The Statement of Claim was filed on July 20, 2017. BDO Canada LLP delivered a Notice of Intent to Defend on November 29, 2017.

The parties attended an early stage mediation. No settlement was reached.

The certification motion was heard by the Honourable Justice Paul Perell on December 9 and 10, 2019. On January 8, 2020, the Court released its decision denying certification of the class action. We have appealed the Court’s decision. The appeal was heard by the Divisional Court on February 1, 2021. The Divisional Court release its decision on April 19, 2021 dismissing the appeal. We are considering next steps, and any updates will be posted on this website.
