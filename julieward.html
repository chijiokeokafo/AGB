Julie Ward

Julie Ward is an associate lawyer at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Julie obtained her Juris Doctorate at the University of Calgary, where she served as an editor and co-editor-in-chief of the Alberta Law Review. During her law degree she was the only student selected to clinical with both the Alberta Human Rights Commission and the Alberta Utilities Commission, where she worked closely with commission counsel. As a law student Julie was an active volunteer for Pro-Bono Students of Canada and represented the complainant in a successful discrimination claim before the Alberta Human Rights Commission.

Before joining the firm, Julie worked as a summer student for the Legal Education Society of Alberta and Torys LLP, where she later completed her articles. More recently, she studied her LL.M. in International Business Law at Osgoode Hall, York University, and anticipates convocation from this program in the fall of 2021.

Throughout her professional and academic career, Julie has authored or contributed to several publications, including the Alberta Law Review.
