Julia Wilkes

Julia Wilkes is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Julia maintains a general civil litigation practice. She acts for clients in corporate and commercial disputes, class actions, securities litigation, and in defence of directors and officers, accountants, and legal professionals. Julia also maintains an administrative law practice and a public law practice acting as counsel to regulatory bodies to restrain unlicensed conduct and provides opinions to integrity commissioners.

Julia has acted as counsel in the Court of Appeal for Ontario and Superior Court of Justice. She regularly appears before administrative tribunals including the University Tribunal at the University of Toronto, the Ontario Securities Commission, and the Health Professions Appeal and Review Board.

Before joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Julia completed her articles at Paliare Roland Rosenberg Rothstein LLP and practiced as an associate at Wardle Daley Bernstein Bieber LLP.

Julia is committed to enhancing legal education by ensuring that students have an opportunity to interact with members of the bar. She is an active volunteer in the University of Toronto Faculty of Law Alumni-Student Mentorship Program and maintains ties to Downtown Legal Services, the student legal clinic at the University of Toronto. Julia volunteers with the Education Law Project at Pro Bono Law Ontario and is a past member of the University of Toronto HIV/AIDS Research Ethics Board.
