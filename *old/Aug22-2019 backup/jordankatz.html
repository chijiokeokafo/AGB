Jordan Katz

Jordan Katz is an associate at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Jordan maintains a broad litigation practice, including complex contractual and tort disputes in a wide range of industries, administrative and regulatory matters, internal investigations, and criminal cases. He has appeared before all levels of Court in Ontario, the Discipline Committee of the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Ontario, and other administrative tribunals.

Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Jordan practiced as a litigation associate at McCarthy Tetrault LLP, where he also completed his articles.

Jordan received his law degree from the University of Toronto. He was awarded the Stephanie Fleur Couzin Mooting Award for outstanding performance in and commitment to student advocacy, and also received a Blakes Scholar award. He also served on the Senior Board of the University of Toronto Faculty of Law Review.

While in law school, Jordan worked as a family law case worker at Downtown Legal Services legal aid clinic, representing clients at the Ontario Court of Justice. He has continued to provide pro bono assistance to the clinic's criminal defence program.
