Gord McGuire

Gord McGuire is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Gord acts for individuals and businesses in all manner of civil and commercial litigation. His areas of practice include insurance coverage disputes, business and contractual disputes, employment law, real estate litigation, defamation, partnership and shareholder disputes, and professional negligence claims.

Gord has appeared as lead or co-counsel in more than 50 reported decisions in the Superior Court of Justice, Divisional Court, and Court of Appeal, and has conducted several multi-week trials in Superior Court.

Gord graduated from Dalhousie Law School as the Gold Medalist of his class, and was the recipient of numerous academic awards and distinctions. After articling at Fraser Milner Casgrain LLP (now Dentons), Gord was selected as one of two Ontario lawyers to receive the Fox Scholarship, through which he spent a year working alongside barristers in London, England.

Gord actively contributes to legal publications and education. He is an Adjunct Professor at the University of Western Ontario, where he teaches Insurance Law, and is a contributing author for Lexis Practice Advisor Canada on the subject of Insurance Broker’s Negligence. His views are frequently published in the popular press, including the Globe & Mail, National Post and Toronto Star.

As the father of two boys under 4, Gord regularly acts as lead or co-counsel on complex, multiparty disputes involving enforcement of bed times, injunctions against food-throwing, and interpretation of bedtime stories.
