Julia Wilkes

Julia Wilkes is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Julia maintains a general civil litigation practice. She acts for clients in corporate and commercial disputes, class actions, securities litigation, and in defence of directors and officers, accountants, and legal professionals. Julia acts for regulatory bodies to restrain unlicensed conduct and maintains an administrative law practice acting for students engaged in disciplinary proceedings and as counsel to integrity commissioners.

Julia has acted as counsel in the Court of Appeal for Ontario and Superior Court of Justice. She has also appeared before regulatory and administrative tribunals including the Ontario Securities Commission, Health Professions Appeal and Review Board, and the University Tribunal at the University of Toronto.
