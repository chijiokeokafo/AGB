Geoff Adair, Partner

Geoff Adair is one of Canada’s leading trial and appellate lawyers.

Geoff Adair is the author of On Trial, Advocacy Skills Law and Practice, a leading text on advocacy described by Justice Renaud as “a treasure trove for litigators”. The book has been cited by courts across Canada on various points of advocacy. He is a past president of the Canadian Bar Association – Ontario Branch Civil Litigation Section and served for many years as an instructor in the Law Society of Upper Canada Bar Admission Course – Civil Litigation Section. He is a regular and frequent lecturer at continuing education programs including the Law Society of Upper Canada Special Lecture series as well as programs and trial demonstrations promoted by the Ontario Trial Lawyers Association, the Advocates Society and the Canadian Bar Association – Ontario Branch.

Geoff Adair has tried or argued upon appeal an astounding breadth of cases before judges or juries over his many years at the Bar ranging from murder trials to income tax appeals to the most sophisticated of commercial litigation matters. His practice now focuses upon commercial litigation cases, professional liability and product liability claims whether for plaintiffs or defendants, substantial insurance coverage disputes including directors’ and officers’ liability insurance coverage cases for insurers and insureds and serious tort litigation including defamation, bad faith, wrongful death or significant personal injury matters.
