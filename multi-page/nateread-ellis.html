Nathaniel Read-Ellis

Nate Read-Ellis is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Nate has a broad commercial litigation practice across a wide range of industries, including in the telecommunications, renewable energy, commercial real estate, financial and mining industries. His experience includes major contractual disputes, competition disputes, tax litigation, shareholder disputes, and advertising litigation.

Nate has represented clients at all levels of court in Ontario and in front of other judicial bodies. Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Nate worked for several years in the litigation group at Davies Ward Philips & Vineberg LLP. Nate received his law degree from the University of Toronto, where he received a number of awards for his academic achievement.

Nate has volunteered actively with Big Brothers and Big Sisters of Toronto since 2015
