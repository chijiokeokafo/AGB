Alex Fidler-Wener

Alex maintains a broad civil litigation practice, acting in corporate/commercial disputes, professional regulation and liability, securities matters, administrative proceedings, internal investigations, civil sexual assault claims. Alex has appeared before the Ontario Superior Court of Justice and the Court of Appeal for Ontario.

Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Alex was an associate with a prominent civil litigation firm. Alex spent her articles clerking for the judges of the Court of Appeal for Ontario. She spent her summers as a law student working for one of Canada’s leading full service law firms and the Legal Services Branch of Ontario’s Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care.

While studying at the University of Toronto Faculty of Law, Alex was active in pro bono legal work as a case worker at Advocates for Injured Workers and a researcher with the Faculty’s International Human Rights Program.
