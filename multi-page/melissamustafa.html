Melissa Mustafa

Melissa Mustafa is an associate at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Melissa practices in all areas of civil litigation, with a keen interest in commercial and employment related disputes. Melissa has appeared before the Ontario Superior Court of Justice and the Small Claims Court.

Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Melissa completed her Practical Legal Training (Australian articling) at a prominent general practice firm in Sydney’s Central Business District. In 2014, Melissa was added to the roll of Solicitors/Lawyers in the state of New South Wales. Melissa relocated to Canada in the same year. Called in both Australia and Canada, she is a multi-jurisdictional lawyer.

Melissa is an active volunteer with Pro Bono Ontario. She also volunteers at the legal clinic run by the Canadian Centre for Men and Families.
