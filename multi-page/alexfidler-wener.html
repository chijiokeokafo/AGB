Alex Fidler-Wener

Alex Fidler-Wener is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Alex maintains a broad civil litigation practice. She acts in corporate and commercial disputes, professional regulation and liability, securities litigation, administrative proceedings, internal investigations, and civil sexual assault claims. Alex has appeared before the Ontario Superior Court of Justice and the Court of Appeal for Ontario, and the Discipline Committee of the College of Physicians and Surgeons.

Prior to joining Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP, Alex was an associate at Wardle Daley Bernstein Bieber LLP. Alex spent her articles clerking for the judges of the Court of Appeal for Ontario. She spent her summers as a law student with Blake, Cassels & Graydon LLP and the Legal Services Branch of Ontario’s Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care.

Alex is committed to the provision of pro bono legal services. While studying at the University of Toronto Faculty of Law, Alex worked as a case worker at Advocates for Injured Workers and a researcher with the Faculty’s International Human Rights Program. Alex is currently an active participant in Pro Bono Ontario’s Divisional Court Amicus Program and Free Legal Advice Hotline.
