Gord McGuire

Gord McGuire is a partner at Adair Goldblatt Bieber LLP.

Gord acts for individuals and businesses in all manner of civil, commercial, employment and insurance litigation. His areas of practice include business and contractual disputes, employment law, partnership and shareholder disputes, insurance claims litigation, and professional negligence claims, insurance brokers, and real estate agents.

Gord has appeared as lead or co-counsel in more than 40 reported decisions in the Superior Court of Justice, Divisional Court, and Court of Appeal, and has conducted several multi-week trials in Superior Court.

Gord graduated from Dalhousie Law School as the Gold Medalist of his class, and was the recipient of numerous academic awards and distinctions. After articling at Fraser Milner Casgrain LLP (now Dentons), Gord was selected as one of two Ontario lawyers to receive the Fox Scholarship, through which he spent a year working alongside barristers in London, England.

Gord actively contributes to legal publications and education. Gord recently authored an electronic chapter entitled “Insurance Broker’s Negligence” for Lexis Practice Advisor Canada, and his views have been quoted in the National Post, Globe & Mail, Law Times, and The Lawyers Weekly. Gord is also a Director of the Empire club of Canada, one of Canada’s oldest speaking clubs and regular host to the country’s business and political leaders.
